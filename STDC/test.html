Courses & Career Opportunities

Explore various programs, exams, and skill-based certifications

1. General Studies & Science (UPSC, SSC, Civil Services, State PSC)

Eligibility:

  • Graduation from a recognized university (any stream, but science/general studies help).
  • Age: 21–32 years for UPSC/Civil Services (varies by exam).

Opportunities:

  • IAS, IPS, IFS
  • State PSC cadres: SDM, DSP, Tehsildar
  • SSC: Auditors, Inspectors, Assistant roles

2. UGC NET / CSIR NET / DBT / ICMR

Eligibility:

  • Master’s degree with 55% (50% for SC/ST/OBC/PH).
  • Final-year master’s students can apply.

Opportunities:

  • Assistant Professor / Lecturer
  • Junior Research Fellowship (JRF)
  • Teaching & academic administration

3. CAT / MAT / XAT (MBA/PGDM)

Eligibility:

  • Bachelor’s degree (min. 50% marks)

Opportunities:

  • MBA/PGDM admissions
  • Careers in Finance, Marketing, HR, Consulting, Analytics

4. IBPS PO / Clerk

Eligibility:

  • Graduation in any discipline
  • Clerk: 20–28 yrs, PO: 20–30 yrs
  • Proficiency in local language (for Clerk)

Opportunities:

  • Clerk: Data entry, customer service, accounts
  • PO: Officer roles → Branch Manager

5. Programming & Data Science

Eligibility:

  • Graduation (preferably CS/IT) or short certifications

Opportunities:

  • Software Developer, Data Analyst
  • Data Scientist, Business Analyst
  • Research roles

6. Cybersecurity & Networking

Eligibility:

  • Degree/diploma in CS/IT or certifications (CompTIA Security+, CEH, etc.)

Opportunities:

  • Cybersecurity Analyst, Network Engineer
  • Security Consultant, Ethical Hacker

7. Food Corporation of India (FCI)

Eligibility:

  • Varies by post (Graduate degree, BSc Agriculture, BTech Food Tech, etc.)
  • Age: 18–28 yrs (some 35 yrs)

Opportunities:

  • Manager, Technical Officer
  • Depot Officer, Engineer, Accounts Manager

8. Digital Marketing Certifications

Eligibility:

  • Open to graduates from any field
  • No prerequisites (HubSpot, Google, Meta certifications)

Opportunities:

  • Digital Marketing Manager, SEO Specialist
  • Content Marketer, Social Media Manager

9. GATE (M.Tech / PSU Jobs)

Eligibility:

  • Bachelor’s in Engg/Tech/Science
  • Valid GATE score

Opportunities:

  • M.Tech admissions (IITs/NITs)
  • PSU jobs: IOCL, NTPC, SAIL, ONGC

10. CLAT – PG

Eligibility:

  • LLB degree or equivalent

Opportunities:

  • LLM admissions
  • Legal academia, judiciary, law firms

11. ICAR AIEEA – PG

Eligibility:

  • Bachelor’s in Agriculture or allied disciplines

Opportunities:

  • Master’s admissions in agriculture universities
  • Research, teaching, agri-enterprises

12. RRB Examinations

Eligibility:

  • Graduate degree/diploma for technical posts
  • Age: 18–33 yrs

Opportunities:

  • Railway jobs: Junior Engineer, Station Master
  • Commercial Apprentice, Clerk, etc.
