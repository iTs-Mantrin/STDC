About Us

The Student Training and Development Centre (STDC) at SGT University is a dedicated initiative to empower students with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities needed to excel in competitive examinations and professional careers.

STDC serves as a one-stop hub for students preparing for national and state-level examinations such as UPSC, SSC, Civil Services, State PSC, CAT/MAT/XAT, IBPS PO/Clerk, UGC NET, GATE, CLAT, RRB, ICAR AIEEA, and more. In addition, it offers specialized training in emerging fields like Programming & Data Science, Cybersecurity, Digital Marketing, and Food Corporation of India (FCI) exams.

At STDC, we believe in “Prepare Today, Succeed Tomorrow.” With over 400+ registrations, the Centre is committed to creating a supportive learning ecosystem that not only enhances academic and exam performance but also builds the confidence and skills required for lifelong success.
